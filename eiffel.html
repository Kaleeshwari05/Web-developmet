Eiffel Tower


Using Inline

The Eiffel Tower is one of the world's most iconic landmarks, standing proudly on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France.
Designed by engineer Gustave Eiffel and completed in 1889, it was originally constructed as the centerpiece for the 1889 Exposition Universelle (World's Fair)
to celebrate the centennial of the French Revolution. Despite initial criticism for its industrial design, the tower has become a beloved symbol of France and a marvel of engineering.
